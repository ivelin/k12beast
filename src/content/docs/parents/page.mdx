# K12Beast for Parents 👪

K12Beast empowers parents to guide their child’s K-12 education with **AI-powered tutoring**. This guide helps you support your child’s learning through personalized lessons, progress reports, and tutor communication. Let’s make education fun and effective! 📚

## Why K12Beast? 🌟

K12Beast offers tools to enhance your child’s learning:
- **Personalized Lessons**: AI tailors content to your child’s needs.
- **Interactive Quizzes**: Engaging assessments to reinforce skills.
- **Progress Reports**: Track your child’s growth with detailed insights.
- **Tutor Messaging**: Collaborate with tutors to support your child.

## Example: Planning a Lesson 📝

Here’s how you can interact with K12Beast’s AI to plan a math lesson for your child:

<div className="p-4 bg-gray-100 rounded-lg my-4">
  <p><strong>You:</strong> Can you create a math lesson for my 5th grader focusing on fractions?</p>
  <p><strong>K12Beast AI:</strong> Absolutely! I’ve generated a lesson on fractions, including a video tutorial, 5 practice problems, and a quiz. Would you like to review it before assigning it to your child? 🧮</p>
  <p><strong>You:</strong> Yes, please send it to me!</p>
  <p><strong>K12Beast AI:</strong> Lesson sent to your parent account. You can assign it now or adjust the difficulty. Ready to proceed? ✅</p>
</div>

## Getting Started 🚀

1. **Sign Up**: Create a parent account at [Sign Up](/public/signup).
2. **Link Your Child**: Connect your child’s account to access their lessons.
3. **Explore Lessons**: Review AI-generated lessons and quizzes.
4. **View Reports**: Check progress reports to monitor growth.

For step-by-step setup, see [Getting Started](/public/docs/getting-started).
