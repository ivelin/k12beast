# K12Beast for Parents 👪

K12Beast helps you support your child’s K-12 education with AI-powered tutoring. This guide shows how to use personalized lessons, interactive quizzes, and tutor communication to enhance learning. Let’s make education engaging and effective 📚

## Why K12Beast? 🌟

K12Beast offers:
- Personalized Lessons: AI tailors content to your child’s needs.
- Interactive Quizzes: Fun assessments to build skills.
- Tutor Messaging: Connect with tutors to support learning.

> **Tip**: Motivate your child to complete quizzes to boost confidence ✨

## Example: Requesting a Lesson 📝

Here’s how you can use K12Beast’s chat to request a lesson:

- **You**: Can you help with a 5th-grade math lesson on fractions? See attached image of a problem he struggles with. 📸
- **K12Beast AI**: Yes, the image illustrates a 5th grade faction problem... Following is a lesson presented in an age appropriate style... You can share and assign this lesson to your child for continued work, examples and quizzes? 🧮

## Getting Started 🚀

1. Sign Up: Create an account at [Sign Up](/public/signup).
2. Link Your Child: Connect your child’s account to access their lessons.
3. Explore Lessons: View AI-suggested lessons and quizzes.
4. Message Tutors: Communicate with tutors to support your child.

See [Getting Started](/public/docs/getting-started) for detailed setup instructions.

## Supporting Your Child’s Learning 🎓

### Encourage Quizzes 😊
Encourage your child to complete quizzes daily.

### Message Tutors 💬
Use the chat to coordinate with tutors.

### Explore Subjects 📚
Check available subjects in [Subjects](/public/docs/subjects).
