## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### What is K12Beast?
K12Beast is an **AI-powered tutoring platform** designed for K-12 students, offering personalized lessons, quizzes, and progress tracking for parents and educators.

### How does K12Beast personalize learning?
Our AI analyzes student inputs (text or images) to create **tailored lessons** and **quizzes** that address individual learning gaps, ensuring effective growth.

### Is K12Beast mobile-friendly?
Yes! K12Beast is fully responsive, providing a seamless experience on **smartphones**, **tablets**, and **desktops**. Learn anywhere, anytime! 📱💻

### Who can use K12Beast?
K12Beast is built for **students**, **parents**, **teachers**, and **tutors**, with dedicated guides to maximize its benefits for each group.
