# Getting Started with K12Beast

Welcome to K12Beast! This guide helps you set up your account and begin your learning journey as a student, parent, or teacher.

## First Steps

1. Go to [/public/signup](/public/signup).
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Verify your email to activate your account.

4. Log in at [/public/login](/public/login).
5. Navigate to [/chat](/chat).
6. Submit a problem to start a personalized tutoring session.
