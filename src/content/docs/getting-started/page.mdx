# Getting Started with K12Beast

Welcome to K12Beast! This guide helps you set up your account and begin your learning journey.

## Sign Up

1. Go to [/public/signup](/public/signup).
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Verify your email to activate your account.

## Start a Session

1. Log in at [/public/login](/public/login).
2. Navigate to [/chat](/chat).
3. Submit a problem to start a personalized tutoring session.
