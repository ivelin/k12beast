## Step-by-Step Setup

Follow these steps to get started with K12Beast:

### 1. Sign Up or Log In

- Visit the [Sign Up](/public/signup) page to create a free account or [Log In](/public/login) if you already have one.
- Accounts are required to save your progress and access personalized features.
- **Tip**: Parents and teachers can create accounts to monitor student progress.

### 2. Start a Chat Session

- Navigate to the [Chat](/chat) page to begin a new tutoring session.
- Submit a problem (via text or image) to receive a tailored lesson from our AI.
- **Example**: Students can input a math problem like "What is 2 + 2?" to get started.

### 3. Explore K12Beast Features

- **Personalized Lessons**: Receive AI-generated lessons tailored to your problem.
- **Interactive Quizzes**: Test your knowledge with fun, adaptive quizzes.
- **Progress Tracking**: Parents and teachers can view session history at [/history](/history).
- **Session Sharing**: Share sessions with parents or teachers for review.
