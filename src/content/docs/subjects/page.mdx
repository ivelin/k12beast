# Subjects

Browse the subjects available in K12Beast, from math to science and more.

## Available Subjects

K12Beast offers tutoring in core K12 subjects, including:
- **Mathematics**: Algebra, geometry, calculus, and more.
- **Science**: Biology, chemistry, physics, and earth science.
- **English**: Reading, writing, and literature.
- **Social Studies**: History, geography, and civics.

To start a subject-specific tutoring session, visit [/chat](/chat) after logging in.
