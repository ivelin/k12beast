# K12Beast Subjects 📚

K12Beast supports a range of K-12 subjects to help students excel in their studies. This guide introduces subjects like math, science, English, and social studies, using personalized lessons, interactive quizzes, and image uploads. Let’s explore your learning options 🌟

## Supported Subjects 🌍

K12Beast covers:
- **Math**: From basic arithmetic to algebra and geometry.
- **Science**: Topics like biology, chemistry, and physics.
- **Language Arts**: Reading, writing, and grammar skills (English, Spanish and other languages).
- **Social Studies**: History, geography, and civics.

> **Tip**: Try a quiz in each subject to test your skills ✨
> **Tip**: Try prompting in different natural languages you are fluent in and want your child or student to learn these languages. ✨

## Example: Exploring a Subject 📝

Here’s how you can use K12Beast’s chat to dive into a subject:

- **You**: Can you help with a 5th-grade science lesson on ecosystems? Here’s a diagram my teacher gave me. 📸
- **K12Beast AI**: Thanks for the diagram. I suggest an ecosystems lesson with practice questions and a quiz. Ready to start? 🔬
- **You**: Yes, let’s go.
- **K12Beast AI**: Lesson started. Work through the questions and quiz to master ecosystems. Share your progress with your teacher when ready ✅
