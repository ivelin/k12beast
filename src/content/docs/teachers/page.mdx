# K12Beast for Teachers 👩‍🏫

K12Beast supports K-12 teachers in creating engaging classroom experiences with AI-powered tutoring. This guide shows how to use personalized lessons, interactive quizzes, and image uploads to enhance student learning. Let’s make your classroom thrive 📚

## Why K12Beast? 🌟

K12Beast offers:
- Personalized Lessons: AI tailors content to each student’s needs.
- Interactive Quizzes: Fun assessments to reinforce classroom topics.
- Image Uploads: Let students share problems for customized support.

> **Tip**: Assign quizzes to review key concepts before tests ✨

## Example: Supporting a Lesson 📝

Here’s how you can use K12Beast’s chat to support a classroom lesson:

- **You**: I need a 7th-grade history lesson on the American Revolution. Attached is a PDF with outline of my lesson plan. 📸
- **K12Beast AI**: Based on the image, I suggest the following lesson with practice questions on the American Revolution... If you approve, you can share with your class for each student to work on individually and present their quiz results. 📜

## Getting Started 🚀

1. Sign Up: Create an account at [Sign Up](/public/signup).
2. Explore Lessons: Access AI-suggested lessons for your curriculum.
3. Assign lessons and quizzes: Use the chat to create personalized lessons and quizzes for your students.
4. Share with Students: Use the share feature to distribute lessons and quizzes.
5. Monitor Progress: Track student performance through quiz results and chat interactions.

See [Getting Started](/public/docs/getting-started) for detailed setup instructions.

## Enhancing Your Classroom 🎓

### Assign Quizzes 😊
Use quizzes to reinforce daily lessons and assess understanding.

### Use Image Uploads 📸
Encourage students to upload homework questions for personalized help.
