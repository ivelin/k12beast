# Teachers Guide

Integrate K12Beast into your teaching with personalized lessons and quizzes.

## Getting Started

1. **Sign Up**: Create an account at [/signup](/signup).
2. **Start a Session**: Begin a session at [/chat/new](/chat/new) by submitting a problem.
3. **Track Progress**: Monitor student progress through shared sessions.
