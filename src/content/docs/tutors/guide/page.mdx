# Tutoring Guide 🧑‍🏫

K12Beast supercharges your K-12 tutoring with AI-powered tools! This detailed guide offers advanced strategies to create engaging sessions, foster student independence, and assess progress effectively. Our documentation is WCAG 2.1 compliant, ensuring accessibility for all users. Let’s take your tutoring to the next level! 📚

## Why This Guide? 🌟

This guide builds on [K12Beast for Tutors](/public/docs/tutors) by providing in-depth techniques to:

- *Optimize Lessons*: Craft sessions for specific student goals.
- *Boost Engagement*: Use quizzes and examples creatively.
- *Assess Progress*: Analyze student readiness with precision.
- *Foster Independence*: Encourage self-paced learning via shared sessions.
- *Maximize Flexibility*: Tutor seamlessly across devices.

> **Tip**: Tailor sessions to student needs for maximum impact! ✨

## Example: Advanced Tutoring Session 📝

Here’s how to create a targeted lesson on [Chat](/chat):

- **You**: Design a 9th-grade English lesson on analyzing poetry, focusing on metaphors. 📜
- **K12Beast AI**: Here’s a lesson on poetic metaphors. Share this session from [Chat](/chat) with your student to clone and explore examples and quizzes at their own pace! 📖

## Advanced Tutoring Strategies 🚀

1. **Diagnose Student Needs**  
   - Start sessions by submitting specific problems on [Chat](/chat], like “Solve x^2 - 4 = 0” or “Explain photosynthesis”.  
   - Use AI feedback to identify gaps (e.g., misunderstanding variables).

2. **Create Targeted Sessions**  
   - Craft lessons addressing weaknesses, e.g., “Practice factoring quadratics”.  
   - Share the session link from [Chat](/chat] for students to clone and work on [Chat](/chat].

3. **Engage with Dynamic Content**  
   - Request extra examples on [Chat](/chat] to deepen understanding.  
   - Assign quizzes to test mastery, aiming for >90% readiness.

4. **Assess and Adjust**  
   - Ask students to share cloned session links from [Chat](/chat].  
   - Review quiz scores and readiness to refine future sessions.

See [Getting Started](/public/docs/getting-started) for setup instructions.

## Cloning and Student Independence 📋

When you share a session:

- Students open the link and log into their K12Beast account via [Log In](/public/login].
- They click the “Clone” button to copy the session to their personal space.
- The cloned session opens on [Chat](/chat] for immediate work on lessons, examples, and quizzes.
- Cloned sessions appear in their [History](/history], like any session they own.
- Students share progress from [Chat](/chat] to show their achievements.

## Tracking Student Sessions 📜

- Visit [History](/history] to review your created sessions, like tailored lessons.
- Request student-cloned session links from [Chat](/chat] to track progress.
- Use readiness scores (>90%) and quiz results to guide your tutoring plan.

## Expert Tutoring Techniques 🎓

- *Scaffold Learning*: Start with simple problems, then increase complexity on [Chat](/chat].  
- *Encourage Reflection*: Ask students to share sessions and discuss their progress.  
- *Customize Quizzes*: Request quizzes on [Chat](/chat] for specific skills, like verb conjugation.  
- *Support All Subjects*: Explore K-12 topics in [Subjects](/public/docs/subjects].  
- *Build Motivation*: Celebrate >90% readiness with positive feedback.

## Troubleshooting

- *Student not cloning?* Ensure they’re logged in and click “Clone” on the shared session.  
- *Progress not shared?* Remind students to share cloned session links from [Chat](/chat].  
- *Need help?* Use the feedback link on the [main page](/) or email support@k12beast.com.

## Get Started 🐾

Jump in! Create a session on [Chat](/chat] or explore [K12Beast for Tutors](/public/docs/tutors). Master tutoring with K12Beast!
