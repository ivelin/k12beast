# Tutors 🧑‍🏫

K12Beast is your AI-powered partner for delivering personalized K-12 tutoring! This guide shows how to use tailored lessons, quizzes, and session sharing to support your students’ learning. Our documentation is WCAG 2.1 compliant, ensuring accessibility for all users. Let’s make tutoring impactful! 📚

## Why K12Beast for Tutors? 🌟

K12Beast equips tutors with tools to enhance one-on-one or small-group tutoring:

- *Custom Lessons*: AI creates lessons matched to student needs.
- *Engaging Quizzes*: Reinforce skills with fun assessments.
- *Session Sharing*: Share sessions for students to clone and practice.
- *Progress Tracking*: Monitor student growth via shared sessions.
- *Flexible Access*: Tutor from any device, anywhere.

> **Tip**: Share sessions to empower students to learn independently! ✨

## Example: Tutoring Session 📝

Here’s how to create a lesson on [Chat](/chat):

- **You**: Prepare an 8th-grade algebra lesson on solving linear equations. 🧮
- **K12Beast AI**: Here’s a lesson on linear equations. Share this session from [Chat](/chat) with your student to clone and work on examples and quizzes at their own pace! 📊

## How Tutors Use K12Beast 🚀

1. **Sign Up or Log In**  
   Create a free account at [Sign Up](/public/signup) or [Log In](/public/login) to start sessions.

2. **Create a Tutoring Session**  
   - Go to [Chat](/chat) and submit a topic or problem (text or image), like “Simplify 2x + 3 = 7”.  
   - Get a personalized lesson for your student.

3. **Share with Students**  
   - From [Chat](/chat), share the session link with your student (e.g., via email or messaging).  
   - Students clone the session to work in their personal app space on [Chat](/chat].

4. **Track Student Progress**  
   - Ask students to share their cloned session links from [Chat](/chat] when ready.  
   - Review quiz scores and readiness (>90% recommended) to tailor your tutoring.

See [Getting Started](/public/docs/getting-started) for setup instructions.

## Cloning and Student Engagement 📋

When you share a session:

- Students open the link and log into their K12Beast account via [Log In](/public/login].
- They click the “Clone” button to copy the session to their personal space.
- The cloned session opens on [Chat](/chat] for immediate work on lessons, examples, and quizzes.
- Cloned sessions appear in their [History](/history], like any session they own.
- Students share progress from [Chat](/chat] to show their results.

## Managing Tutoring Sessions 📜

- Visit [History](/history] to review your created sessions, like lessons for students.
- Request student-cloned session links from [Chat](/chat] to track progress.
- Use shared sessions to assess readiness and adjust your tutoring approach.

## Tutoring Strategies 🎓

- *Personalize Sessions*: Create sessions based on student weaknesses, like fractions or grammar.  
- *Encourage Practice*: Motivate students to complete quizzes for >90% readiness.  
- *Reinforce Learning*: Share sessions for extra practice between sessions.  
- *Cover Topics*: Explore K-12 subjects in [Subjects](/public/docs/subjects].  
- *Build Confidence*: Recognize progress when students share sessions.  
- *Learn More*: See [Tutoring Guide](/public/docs/tutors/guide) for advanced techniques.

## Troubleshooting

- *Student not cloning?* Ensure they’re logged in and click “Clone” on the shared session.  
- *Progress not shared?* Remind students to share cloned session links from [Chat](/chat].  
- *Need help?* Use the feedback link on the [main page](/) or email support@k12beast.com.

## Get Started 🐾

Dive in! Create a session on [Chat](/chat] or explore [K12Beast for Tutors](/public/docs/tutors). Master tutoring with K12Beast!
