# K12Beast for Tutors 🧑‍🏫

K12Beast empowers K-12 tutors to deliver engaging tutoring sessions with AI-powered tools. This guide shows how to use personalized lessons, interactive quizzes, and image uploads to support your students. Let’s enhance your tutoring sessions 📚

## Why K12Beast? 🌟

K12Beast offers:
- Personalized Lessons: AI tailors content to each student’s needs.
- Interactive Quizzes: Fun assessments to reinforce learning.
- Image Uploads: Receive student work for customized support.

> **Tip**: Use quizzes to assess student progress after each session ✨

## Example: Creating a Lesson 📝

Here’s how you can use K12Beast’s chat to create a tutoring lesson:

- **You**: I need a 4th-grade English lesson on grammar. Here’s a student’s writing sample image. 📸
- **K12Beast AI**: Based on the image, I suggest the following grammar lesson... If you approve, share with your student to continue working on the lesson with practice exercises and quizzes. ✍️

## Getting Started 🚀

1. Sign Up: Create an account at [Sign Up](/public/signup).
2. Connect with Students: Link student accounts to share lessons.
3. Explore Lessons: Access AI-suggested lessons for your tutoring sessions.
4. Use Image Uploads: Ask students to upload work for tailored help.

See [Getting Started](/public/docs/getting-started) for detailed setup instructions.

## Supporting Your Students 🎓

### Assign Quizzes 😊
Use quizzes to reinforce lessons and track understanding.

### Use Image Uploads 📸
Have students upload assignments to provide personalized feedback.

### Explore Subjects 📚
Check available subjects in [Subjects](/public/docs/subjects).
