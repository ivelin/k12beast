# Students Guide

Welcome to K12Beast, a platform designed to help K-12 students excel through personalized tutoring. This guide will help you get started.

## Getting Started

1. **Sign Up**: Create an account at [/signup](/signup).
2. **Start a Session**: Begin a new chat session at [/chat/new](/chat/new) to submit problems and receive lessons.
3. **Explore Features**: Request examples, take quizzes, and share sessions with your parents or teachers.
