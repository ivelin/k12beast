# Students Guide

Enhance your learning with K12Beast’s tutoring tools.

## Getting Started

1. **Sign Up**: Create an account at [/signup](/signup).
2. **Start a Session**: Go to [/chat/new](/chat/new) and submit a problem.
3. **Track Progress**: Review your sessions at [/history](/history).
